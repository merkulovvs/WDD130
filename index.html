Kyiv, Ukraine

Kyiv, Ukraine's capital, is a city steeped in history. Ancient golden-domed cathedrals like Saint Sophia Cathedral stand alongside modern art installations and a bustling restaurant scene. The Dnieper River winds through the city, offering scenic walks and boat trips. Despite the current challenges, Kyiv endures as a vibrant center of Ukrainian culture and resilience.
