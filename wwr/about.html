Splash White Water Rafting

We offer you

For over 20 years, Splash White Water Rafting has been the premier white-water rafting company in the region. We take pride in our experienced guides and meticulously maintained rafts, ensuring a safe and thrilling adventure for all. We offer a variety of routes to cater to all levels. From the gentle rapids of the Tisa River, perfect for families and first-timers, to the heart-pounding plunges of the Dnister River, experienced rafters will find their match. Our knowledgeable guides provide comprehensive safety briefings and navigate the rivers with skill, ensuring an unforgettable experience. Whether you crave a scenic float or an adrenaline rush, River Rush Adventures has a white-water rafting adventure waiting for you.

History

Splash began as a dream in 1998, when two college buddies, Matt and Lisa, took a life-changing white-water rafting trip. Inspired by the exhilarating rapids and stunning scenery, they vowed to share the experience. Starting with a single raft on a local river, Splash has grown into a respected company. Over the years, they've expanded their routes, honed their expertise, and remain dedicated to providing unforgettable adventures for all.
